Dealing with bugs

At the start of a cycle, the Release Team will discuss which ISO Testing Weeks we will participate in. Then, during a Community Meeting, members of Xubuntu Team will discuss and then ratify Xubuntu's participation during the cycle.

Along with general testing of our OS, dealt with further in Tests exploratoires and Utiliser les PPA de développement, further responsibilities lie with ensuring that:

  • Sufficient testing takes place during ISO Testing Weeks

  • Des tests de paquets suffisants ont lieu après les appels aux testeurs

  • Sufficient testing has taken place by Final Release

  • Bugs reported to our trackers are confirmed, or where unable to confirm, further information is requested from the reporter

  • Where appropriate, confirmation of bugs can be asked of members of Xubuntu Team in the team devel IRC channel

Members of the QA team might find it useful subscribing to the Xubuntu Bugs team in order to be aware of bugs being reported by users.
